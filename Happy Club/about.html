About

Happy Club is a social club. This club is popular for the worship of goddess Maa Saraswati that is called "Vasant Panchami" . It celebrate Basant Panchami Every year with joy and peace. This club also arrange a small devotional vigil in the night of the pooja. We do vigil all the night of the pooja. Coming to the immersion of the statue, We do immersion with very maffick.
